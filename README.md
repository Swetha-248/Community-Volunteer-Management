# VolunteerHub – Web-Based Community Volunteer Management System

## Project Title
Development of a Web-Based Community Volunteer Management and Engagement System

## Project Description
VolunteerHub is a web-based application designed to help communities organize and manage volunteer activities. It connects volunteers with community projects such as cleanup drives, disaster relief support, and local infrastructure improvements like street lighting. The system allows organizers to manage events and volunteers while providing real-time updates.

## Objectives
- To simplify the management of community volunteer activities.
- To connect volunteers with community service opportunities.
- To track volunteer participation and event attendance.
- To provide real-time updates and notifications.

## Features
- Volunteer registration and login
- Event creation and management by organizers
- Volunteer sign-up for community events
- Participation and attendance tracking
- Real-time updates and notifications
- Dashboard for organizers and volunteers

## System Outcomes
The final outcome will be a fully functional web-based platform that improves community engagement by simplifying volunteer management. Community organizers can post events, track participation, and coordinate volunteers efficiently, while volunteers can easily find and participate in community service activities.
